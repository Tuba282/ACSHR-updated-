Our Promise

The Alliance of the Civil Society for Human Rights (ACSHR), founded in February 2010 in Karachi, is a national NGO formed by local Pakistani intellectuals and community members. ACSHR supports vulnerable communities in urban and rural Pakistan by providing sustainable livelihood opportunities, education, primary health care, and employment. Registered with the Sub-registrar of Gulshan Iqbal Town, Karachi, ACSHR focuses on sustainable, community-based programs that align with national and international strategies.



Promoting Sustainable Livelihoods and Human Rights for All Communities

Alliance of the Civil Society for Human Rights (ACSHR) is a national NGO founded in February 2010 in Karachi. It aims to support vulnerable communities in Pakistan through sustainable livelihood interventions, offering better education, primary healthcare, and employment opportunities while upholding human rights, peace, and good governance. ACSHR's programs are holistic, people-centered, and community-based, aligning with both international and national humanitarian strategies. Registered with the Sub-registrar of Gulshan Iqbal Town Karachi, ACSHR utilizes local solutions to address community challenges and needs.



Empowering Vulnerable Communities

Providing support through sustainable livelihood interventions. Ensuring better education, healthcare, and employment opportunities.

Holistic Development Programs

Implementing people-centered, community-based solutions. Aligning with international and national humanitarian strategies.

Local Solutions for Global Challenges

Utilizing home-ground strategies to address community needs. Focused on sustainable, long-term development.

Our Objectives

Sustainable Food Security

Initiate sustainable methods to reduce malnutrition and improve self-sufficiency.

Quality Education Access

Increase education access for vulnerable children and improve teaching quality.

Safe Water & Sanitation

Ensure sustainable access to safe water and sanitation for everyone.

Primary Health Care

Expand primary health care access for vulnerable groups.

Environmental Awareness

Promote environmental awareness and advocacy through various initiatives.

Local Capacity Building

Enhance the management and service delivery skills of local organizations.





ACSHR strives to ultimately see a stable and economical strong Pakistani society living in harmony, peace, good governance, rule of law, human rights and sustainable resource management.
