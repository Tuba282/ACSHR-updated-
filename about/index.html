Our Promise

The Alliance of the Civil Society for Human Rights (ACSHR), founded in February 2010 in Karachi, is a national NGO formed by local Pakistani intellectuals and community members. ACSHR supports vulnerable communities in urban and rural Pakistan by providing sustainable livelihood opportunities, education, primary health care, and employment. Registered with the Sub-registrar of Gulshan Iqbal Town, Karachi, ACSHR focuses on sustainable, community-based programs that align with national and international strategies.

Alliance of the Civil Society for Human Rights (ACSHR)

Alliance of the Civil Society for Human Rights (ACSHR) is a national level of NGO. It was founded in February 2010 in Karachi. It is a Humanitarian and development organization by local Pakistani intellectual and community members. The purpose is to provide sustainable livelihoods intervention opportunities to support the most vulnerable communities both in urban and rural areas of Pakistan consequently will have access to better education, adequate primary health care and employment opportunities respect the ideals of human rights, peace, good governance and eventually improved sustainable community development. The Organization is registered from the office of Sub-registrar of Gulshan I Iqbal town Karachi. Subsequently to consultative meetings in Karachi, the founders responded to the urgent need of Pakistani people and made the platform named as Alliance of the Society for Human Rights (ACSHR). ACSHR humanitarian and development intervention programs are holistic, people centered community based and sustainable. Therefore, sustainable livelihood approaches are adopted for all ACSHR strategic and short term programs. ACSHR sustainable livelihood program interventions only add to the current humanitarian and development commitments in Pakistan by utilizing principally home ground solutions to vulnerable community challenges and needs. ACSHR corresponds to the existing international and national humanitarian and development strategies.

Focus Areas





INTERFAITH HARMONY

Strategies for Inter-Faith Harmony

Providing strategies and solutions for Inter-Faith Harmony in accordance with the Basic Islamic Sources.

Social Awareness

Create social awareness and improve collective common sense in the society regarding Inter-Faith Harmony.

Mutual Understanding

Develop mutual understanding among different religious sects of the society.

Security and Justice

Eliminate the sense of insecurity and injustice among the religious minorities.

Social Justice

To ensure social justice and fair distribution of socio-economic resources.

Role of Institutions

Highlight the role of Universities,Judiciary and Legislation Institutions to address the challenges to Inter-Faith Harmony.

Environment of Tolerance

Create an environment of fore-bearance, tolerance and co-existence in the society.

Revival of Islamic Concepts

To suggest the actions and steps for the revival of Islamic concept related to Inter-Faith Harmony.





ACSHR strives to ultimately see a stable and economical strong Pakistani society living in harmony, peace, good governance, rule of law, human rights and sustainable resource management.
